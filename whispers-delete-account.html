Account Deletion Guide - Whispers

If you wish to delete your account with Whispers, you can follow the steps outlined below. Please note that deleting your account will permanently remove all associated data, and this action cannot be undone.

1. How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Request Account Deletion via Email

You can request to delete your account by sending an email to our support team:

  • Email: worldwhispersapp@gmail.com
  • Include your username and the email address associated with your account in the request.

Option 2: Delete Your Account In-App

To delete your account directly within the Whispers app, follow these steps:

  • Open the Whispers app on your device.
  • Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) to open the navigation drawer.
  • Tap the Settings icon.
  • Select Delete Account and Data.
  • Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account.

2. Data Deletion and Retention Policy

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your username (if provided).
  • Your email address.
  • All messages you have posted through the app.

We do not retain any personal data after the account deletion process is completed. However, some non-personal data or anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes or to comply with legal obligations.

3. Additional Retention Period

While most data will be deleted immediately upon your request, there may be a brief retention period for certain data:

  • Backup copies of your data may exist for up to 30 days following your deletion request, after which they will be permanently removed.
  • If you have made any purchases through the app, transaction records will be retained as required by law.

Please note that once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered, and all associated data will be permanently lost.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us at: worldwhispersapp@gmail.com
