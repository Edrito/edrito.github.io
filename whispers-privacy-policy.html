# Privacy Policy for Whispers Last updated: 11/08/2024 ## 1. Introduction Welcome to Whispers ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe user experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information You Provide We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide: - Username (optional) - Email address - Content of messages you post through the App ### 2.2 Location Information While we do not continuously track or store your GPS location, we do use your device's location services when you choose to create a location-based marker. The location of these markers is stored to enable the App's core functionality. ### 2.3 Device Information We do not collect any non-personal information such as device information or usage statistics. ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the App's services: - To create and manage your account - To enable the core functionality of leaving and viewing location-based messages - To respond to your inquiries or provide support We do not use your personal information for any other purposes. ## 4. Data Storage and Security We use Firebase, a service provided by Google, to store user account information and message data. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. ## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties. Your posted messages are visible to other users of the App, and users can filter messages by type. ## 6. User Controls and Choices You have the following rights regarding your data: - You can delete individual messages you have posted - You can delete all your data and your account at any time - You can choose not to provide a username or use a pseudonym ## 7. Children's Privacy Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. ## 9.
